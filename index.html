Fast Food

Fast food is a staple of modern life, providing quick, convenient, and often inexpensive meals that cater to the fast-paced lifestyle many people lead today.

From burgers and fries to pizza, fried chicken, and even salads, the fast food industry has become a global phenomenon. But while it's easy to grab a meal on the go, the rise of fast food has significant implications for our health, culture, and economy.
